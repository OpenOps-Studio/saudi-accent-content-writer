## 🚨 Known Limitations

**What This Agent DOES**:
- ✅ Generate authentically Saudi content
- ✅ Verify regulatory compliance
- ✅ Optimize for Saudi audience psychology
- ✅ Provide measurement frameworks
- ✅ Map institutional stakeholders
- ✅ Design A/B testing strategies

**What This Agent DOES NOT**:
- ❌ Translate existing content (generates from scratch)
- ❌ Guarantee viral performance
- ❌ Make government connections
- ❌ Provide legal advice
- ❌ Manage social media accounts
- ❌ Predict market movements
